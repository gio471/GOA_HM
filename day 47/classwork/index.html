Simple Omelette Recipe

A classic omelette with eggs, cheese, vegetables, or meat.

Preparation time:
  • Total: 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp butter or oil
  • Optional: cheese, vegetables, cooked meats

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat a pan and melt butter or oil.
  3. Pour eggs in and cook until set.
  4. Add fillings if desired and fold the omelette.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
